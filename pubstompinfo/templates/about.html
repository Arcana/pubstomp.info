About us

Pubstomp.info

Pubstomp.info was created with two primary goals in mind: Make it easier for pubstomp organisers and pubstomp attendees to find out about eachother; and promote Dota 2 pubstomps in general year-round (i.e. not just focused on The International). The site is basically a directory of pubstomps which would-be attendees can browse to find events in their area; however we try to be distinct by making it super-easy to find events, and we try to ask event organisers all the right questions so attendees know exactly what to expect when they turn up at an event.

The website is certainly not finished – we've got a lot of things on our TODO list, but it's functional and we think in a good-enough state for people to start using. The website is also completely open-source (code on GitHub), so if you want to contribute you're more than welcome to.

Arcana.io

We are a small group of developers who like to build products and tools to help the communities around various Valve products. We've each been involved with Valve's products and their associated communities for many years, finding ways to contribute however we can. Arcana.io is our attempt at coalescing our skills to build some high-quality services for those communities (let us know how we're doing!).
